Plimo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Plimo is an app for plant watering reminders, plant care records, optional account backup, and plant care questions. This Privacy Policy explains what information Plimo processes and how that information is used.

1. Information We Process

Plimo may process the following information to provide app features.

  • Account information: Firebase anonymous user ID, and Google account identifier or email address if the user chooses to link a Google account
  • App records: plant names, plant types, pot information, growing location, watering, repotting, check records, notes, notification settings, and guide preferences
  • Backup information: plant information, care records, statistics, and guide profile data backed up to the user's account when backup is enabled
  • AI diagnosis information: questions entered by the user, summarized plant care context, and related information needed to generate an AI response
  • Local plant photos: representative plant photos added by the user

Representative plant photos are currently compressed and stored only inside the app's local storage. They are not included in account backup.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app features such as plant registration, care records, reminders, and widgets
  • To provide account backup and restore when the user enables backup
  • To generate AI answers to plant care questions requested by the user
  • To respond to errors, maintain security, and prevent abuse

3. Third-Party Services

Plimo may use the following third-party services.

  • Firebase Authentication: anonymous accounts and optional Google account linking
  • Cloud Firestore: storage of plant information and care records when backup is enabled
  • Google Gemini API: generation of AI answers for plant care questions entered by the user
  • Google Play: app distribution and possible future paid feature processing

Plimo does not sell user data for advertising purposes and does not use advertising SDKs or tracking analytics SDKs.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data stored on the device may be deleted when the user deletes it in the app or removes the app.
  • Account backup data is deleted when the user deletes cloud backup or deletes account-related data in the app settings.
  • Deletion requests sent by email will be processed within a reasonable period after the request is verified.
  • Some records may be retained for a limited period if required for legal obligations, dispute resolution, or security purposes.

For details, please see the Plimo Account and Data Deletion Guide.

5. Security

Plimo uses encrypted connections when transmitting data through Firebase and Google APIs. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Plimo is not primarily directed to children. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us using the email address below.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to app changes, legal requirements, or policy updates. Important changes may be announced through the app or this web page.
