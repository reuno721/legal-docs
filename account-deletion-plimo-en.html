Plimo Account and Data Deletion Guide

Last updated: May 9, 2026

This page explains how Plimo users can delete account connections and related data. Plimo is a plant watering reminder and plant care management app provided by an individual developer.

1. Delete Data Directly in the App

  1. Open the Plimo app.
  2. Tap the settings button in the upper-right corner of the home screen.
  3. Open the Account & App tab.
  4. Go to the Data Management section.
  5. Select Delete account and all data.
  6. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog.

2. Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or cannot delete your data directly, you may request deletion by email.

Email: reuno21@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your request.

  • App name: Plimo
  • A clear statement that you want your account and/or data deleted
  • The Google email address linked to Plimo, if any

3. Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Google account connection information used by Plimo
  • Plant information, care records, statistics, and guide profile data backed up to Cloud Firestore
  • Plant information, care records, and settings stored on the device
  • Representative plant photos stored in the app's local storage on the device

4. Data That Will Not Be Deleted

  • Your Google account itself will not be deleted.
  • Original photos stored separately in your device gallery or other apps will not be deleted.
  • Some records may be retained for a limited period if required for legal obligations, security, or dispute resolution.

5. Delete Only Some Data

You may delete only part of your data without deleting the account connection.

  • Delete a plant or record: delete the item directly in the app.
  • Delete cloud backup: go to Settings > Account & App > Data Management and select cloud backup deletion.
  • Reset device data: go to Settings > Account & App > Data Management and select data reset.

6. Processing Time

Data deleted directly in the app begins deletion immediately. Email deletion requests will be processed within a reasonable period after the request is verified.
